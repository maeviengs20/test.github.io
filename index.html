Hi, Tana here

Growing up, I moved a lot from continent to continent, culture to culture, flavor to flavor, but I always found my favorite crunchy coated peanut snack - different varieties, different flavors - same great crunch. When my family decided to call Maryland our home, I had a craving for those coated peanuts that I've munched all over the world, and began experimenting with my own crunchy coated peanut recipe, this time with explosive flavors.
Throughout high school (WJHS) and college (Go Spiders!), I would make batches of coated peanuts and share them with friends who would try them and ask what they are while asking for more.

Today, I am happy to be able to share my crunchy KABOOM Nuts with the community with so many happy customers. All of our products are handmade from scratch with simple, locally sourced ingredients, and hand packaged with love in our Maryland kitchen.

Our commitment to making explosive KABOOM Nuts with simple ingredients in the very best way possible for the community, while supporting the community is what we're nuts about (first, and only pun on this site, we promise!). Grab a bag while you're here and say hi if you see me around town!

WHAT WE VALUE: Explore. Eat. Explode.

Our mission is to create a snack bursting with explosive flavors but also to create a space to explore and discover - explore a new snack but we hope you continue to explore, not just with us but in life.

Secondly, our carefully chosen ingredients are simple and locally sourced so that you feel good about what you're eating, not to mention it lowers carbon footprint and support local businesses. It cost you more because it cost us more, but it's really good.

Lastly, we take pride in bringing you the most complex, explosive flavors in every bag we create. Light up every snack with XXXXX nuts.
