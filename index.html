Hi, Tana here

Growing up, I moved a lot from continent to continent, culture to culture, flavor to flavor, but I always found my favorite crunchy coated peanut snack, different brands, concoted in local flavors, but same great crunch . When my family decided to call Maryland our home, I had a craving for those coated peanuts that I've munched all over the world, and began experimenting with my own crunchy coated peanut recipe.
Throughout high school (WJHS) and college (Go Spiders!), I would make and share these coated peanuts with friends who would try them and ask what they are while asking for more.

Today, I am happy to be able to share my crunchy, coated peanuts with the community with so many happy customers. All of our products are handmade from scratch with simple, locally sourced ingredients, and hand packaged with love in our Maryland kitchen.

Our commitment to making crunchy coated peanuts with simple ingredients in the very best way possible for the community, while supporting the community is what we're nuts about. It's our reason for being. Grab a bag while you're here and say hi if you see me around town!

OUR MISSION: BETTER WAY TO FEED OUR FUTURE

Our mission is to create delicious and sustainable snacks because we believe there is a better way to feed our future, no sacrifice required. Our carefully chosen ingredients are locally sourced so it lowers carbon footprint and supports local businesses.


Additionally, did you know peanuts...

  1. Have the lowest fresh water footprint of comparable U.S. grown nuts.
  2. Are Nature's "Zero-Waste" plant.
  3. Improves soil by taking nitrogen from the air and produce their own in the ground, which benefits other crops.
